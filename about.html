ABOUT

Felicia Billie Paulson, also known as FakeBand Presents, is a 27-year-old singer-songwriter who enjoys stealing candy from siblings, writing music and camping. Born in Ottawa, Canada, she currently states that she's a citizen of the whole world.

She started studying business studies at college but never finished the course, pursuing musical career instead. In 2012 she was discovered by Fake Records and was offered a deal. 8 months later she went on her first ever tour, promoting her first CD Abstraction Won't Take Me Alive". The album has since sold million of copies.

After a succesful beginning, encouraged, she proceeded to write and record music. That is how her two other albums came to be. Despite being controversial, "All I have is my Bombshell" (2014) sold even more copies than her debut album. "until debt tear us apart" (2016) is by far her most personal album. She sings her heart out about the ecological tragedy her generation was born into.
