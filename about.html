ABOUT

Felicia Billie Paulson, also known as FakeBand Presents, is a 27-year-old singer-songwriter who enjoys stealing candy from siblings, writing music and camping. Born in Ottawa, Canada, she currently states that she's a citizen of the whole world.
She started studying business studies at college but never finished the course, pursing musical career instead. In 2012 she was discovered by Fake Records and was offered a deal. 8 months later she went on her first ever tour, promoting her first CD "Abstraction Won't Take Me Alive".
Since that time, she's released two other albums: "All I have is my Bombshell" (2014) and "On The Same Topic" (2016). In the upcoming months, her 4th CD "darkdawn" will be released.
